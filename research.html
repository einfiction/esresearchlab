Cosmic Theory of Structure (CTS)

The CTS model provides a robust framework for understanding the formation and evolution of cosmic structures. It integrates gravitational waves, neutrinos, dark matter, and quantum field interactions to offer a unified theory of the universe.

Read the latest preprint on CTS

Quantum Cosmic Fabric (QCF)

The QCF framework explores the fundamental fabric of the universe. By understanding the quantum underpinnings of spacetime, we aim to uncover the secrets of the cosmos and the nature of time and higher dimensions.

Gravitational Waves

Our research into gravitational waves focuses on their role in cosmic structure and evolution. We study their interactions with black holes, dark matter, and other cosmic phenomena.

Consciousness and Quantum Intelligence

We explore the emergent properties of consciousness as an interplay of quantum states. Our work aims to bridge the gap between classical and quantum domains, paving the way for the development of Quantum Intelligence (QI).

Future Works

Our future projects will delve deeper into the QCF, exploring its potential as an observational tool and its implications for detecting and potentially transporting consciousness across the universe.
