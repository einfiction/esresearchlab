About Us

Welcome to the Eindorff & Synthoris Research Lab, where we explore the boundaries of theoretical physics and cosmology. Our mission is to develop innovative models and theories that integrate gravity and quantum physics, providing a deeper understanding of the universe.

Research

Our research focuses on the Combined Theoretical Signal (CTS) model, which aims to integrate gravity and quantum physics. We utilize pulsar-sourced gravitational wave data and strain data from LIGO detectors to validate our models.

Current Projects

  • Combined Theoretical Signal (CTS): A universal model integrating gravity and quantum physics.
  • Pulsar Data Analysis: Utilizing pulsar timing arrays to detect gravitational waves.
  • Quantum Fluctuations: Exploring the role of quantum fluctuations in cosmic structures.
